Description

Arabic

  • This Fus'hah Arabic (Quranic Arabic) for beginners to help understand the Quran which in return will help them to have more concentration while praying salaah.
  • There are no prerequisite for this course
  • A completion of the course certificate will be given once they pass the final exam.
  • The duration is 3 years.

  • Timing: 11 am-12 pm

Tajweed

  • To be able to recite the Quran in the proper manner with the correct pronunciation of the Arabic alphabet and by learning the rules of Tajweed. (You can word this better).
  • The students will receive Ijaazah after the recitation of the whole Quran with Tajweed in front of the teacher. The ijaazah will be for the recitation of Hafs from the ways of Shaatibiyyah.
    (اجازة في قراءة حفص عن عاصم من طريق الشاطبية))
  • There is no prerequisite for this course.
  • Duration is 1-2 years depending on how well the students are able to grasp the Tajweed theories and more importantly how well they are able to apply its rulings to their recitation.

  • Timing: 12 pm-1 pm

Fiqh

  • Fiqh class take a deeper look at the massa'il (rulings) of the 5 pillars of Islam. This course will answer many questions people have regarding their salaah, saum, zakaah, hajj, etc.
  • There is no prerequisite for this course.
  • A completion of the course certificate will be given once the students pass the test at te End the course.
  • Duration is 1 year.

  • Timing: 11 am-12 pm
