Description

By the mercy of Allah, we are blessed with multiple Hifz programs in our area, all striving towards one goal. In fact, to meet the demands of the growing community, we need more institutions dedicated to producing a generation that is righteous and serves the Deen of Allah. We ask parents to explore various options and choose a program which best suits your children's needs and helps you in achieving your goal.

Alhamdulillah, our Full Time Hifz program has been successfully operating in Irving for a couple years now under Mufti Naieem. Masha'Allah, about a dozen Huffaz have graduated and many of them are leading Taraweeh in various places. In fact, one of the goals of our program is to prepare students to become capable of comfortably reciting the entire Quran in Taraweeh with minimal mistakes. We believe the only way to produce such Capable Huffaz is through implementing Rigorous Standards, which foster Strong Retention and emphasize Quality Over Quantity.

Our goal is for Huffaz to be able to recite 3-5 Juz from memory in one sitting.

HIFZ PROGRAM KEY HIGHLIGHTS/FEATURES:

  • Daily Sabaq: Students memorize a certain amount according to their capability and are allowed to pass ONLY with zero mistakes.
  • Daily Sabaq Para (Sabaq of previous ~2 weeks): recited ONLY to teacher and assigned a letter grade (A/B/C/F) based on number of mistakes.
  • Daily 1 Juz Manzil (Revision): recited ONLY to the teacher and assigned a letter grade (A/B/C/F) based on number of mistakes.
  • Daily 5 Juz Tilawah: Students individually read 5 Juz with looking.
  • Weekly Dawr on Fri/Sat: Students pair up and recite 3-5 Juz from memory to one another under supervision of the teacher.
  • Zero-mistake Policy for Passing Each Juz: Upon completion of a Juz, students are not allowed to progress until entire Juz is recited to teacher with zero mistakes.
  • Daily Hadith Reading by students: Students read different Ahadith on virtues from Muntakhab Ahadith.
  • Weekly Halaqat: Students are given lessons from Taleemul Haq as well as talks on miscellaneous topics.
  • Memorization of Masnoon Duas
  • 6 days a week Class
  • Supervised Youth Taraweeh in Ramadan: students perform 20-rakah Taraweeh in pairs, reciting 1 Juz nightly. Student who are not Hafiz are able to complete multiple cycles of their Manzil throughout Ramadan. This pair-system allows for students to prepare and individually recite large portions of the Quran in Taraweeh.
  • Extra Time Given to Beginner Students After-School and on Weekends
  • Extra Time Given to Beginner Students After-School and on Weekends
  • Small Student-Teacher Ratio
