Mufti Naieem

Leading Islamic Scholar and Speaker

He has over a decade of experience in leading communities as an Imam and teacher in the United States. After completing a rigorous Alim course along with Ifta training for 13 years under renowned scholars in the sub-continent, he completed post-graduate studies at Jamiah Azhar in Egypt. He is director of Darul Hikmah and currently runs the boy's full time and part time Hifz/Nazirah programs.

Mufti Abdelaziz

Islamic Scholar

He resides in Bangladesh where he serves as an Imam and teacher. He has been staffed by Mufti Naieem to serve as an advisor and provide complementary services for the students. He assists in listening to students recite their Manzil (Revision). He also does one-on-one sessions with students who need additional help. He teaches remotely via Skype on a large flat-screen TV screen (placed beside Mufti Naieem) with a high-quality headset, microphone, and camera. He has been teaching in this manner for the past year and has received great feedback from both students and parents.

Shaikhah Umm Talhah

Islamic Scholar

She was raised in the US, and is an 'Aalimah (Islamic scholar) who comleted her 'Aalimiyyah degree in Darul Uloom Al-Madania, a renowned institution of advance Islamic education in the US. She spent seven years studying the Islamic sciences, thus gaining an in-depth knowledge and understanding in numerous fields, such as Arabic, Adeedah, Quran, Tafseer, Hadeeth, and Fiqh (Islamic jurisprudence). She graduated with an MA in Islamic Theology and BA in Arabic. She She also has 'Ijazaah in Qiraa'aatul 'Asharah fil Kubraa wa Sughraa (10 major varient readings & 10 minor readings). She is currently working on her BSc in Psychology.

She has over 10+ years of experience teaching Islmic Sciences in several different Islamic institutions including Darul Uloom Al-Madania, USA. She manages the Sisters Wing of Darul Hikmah and currently teaches multiple Arabic and Islamic Studies classes for sisters. She is also the assistent teacher for the full-time Hifz Program for girls.

Shaikhah Bint Ruhullah

Islamic Scholar

She was raised in the US, and is an ‘Aalimah (Islamic scholar) who completed her 'Aalimiyyah degree in Jamiatul Kauthar, UK, a renowned institution for advance Islamic studies.
