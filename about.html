About The School

History

Take A Look at How We Begin

By the grace and mercy of Allah, Darul Hikmah or Hikmah Inc. is a 501(C)(3) non-profit organization (Tax ID: 35-2587347) operating in Irving, Texas since 2014 under Mufti Naieem Chowdhury, who has over a decade of experience in leading communities as an Imam and teacher in the United States. After completing a rigorous 'Aalim course along with Ifta training for 13 years under renowned scholars in the sub-continent, he completed post-graduate studies at Jamiah Azhar in Egypt. The sisters/girls wing of Darul Hikmah is managed by Mufti Naieem's wife, Umm Talhah, who is also an 'Aalimah (Islamic scholar) and has Ijaazah in the 10 Qira'at. Alhamdulillah, Darul Hikmah currently has 100+ students enrolled in its full-time/part-time programs. At present, we offer Hifz for both boys and girls separately, Weekday Naazhirah Programs, Weekend Naazhirah Program, Summer Intensives, Arabic, Tajweed, & Fiqh classes for sisters on Saturdays, and ‘Aalimiyyah Preliminary Course. Inshaa’Allah, in future we have plan to start a full-time ‘Aalimiyyah Program for sisters as well as Tafseer and Hadeeth course for sisters on Saturdays.

We are Irving, Texas based School focused on excellence.

We have been offering courses on Quran and Hadith for last several years and have been trying our best to reach out to souls in our community
